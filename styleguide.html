About Me page

About Me

Lizzie, or, Elizabeth if you prefer. I'm a Vancouver-based UX/UI graphic designer for web and app. Currently studying at Simon Fraser University.

Lifestyle

You can probably find me at a cafe on my days off. I'm a big fan of lattes (matcha, especially), and I live to eat... At home, I like to spend time with my family, play video games with my friends, or idie-pixel games

Career-oriented

I specialize in creating digital graphics for promotional purposes, but I find that working with the web is a lot more fun. Mainly because it's an iterative process

Short term plans include practicing use cases of JavaScript, and hopefully getting to learn ReactJS by the end of Summer 2018.
